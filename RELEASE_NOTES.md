# Release Notes — Scanly

## v1.0.0 — Initial Release

### Short version (for Play Console — under 500 chars)

```
Welcome to Scanly! Design beautiful custom QR codes with unique shapes,
colors, gradients, and logos — then scan any QR code instantly with the
built-in scanner. Save your designs, export as PNG, and share to any app.
Fully offline, no accounts required. Dark mode supported.
```

### Long version (for marketing / website / blog)

**Scanly 1.0 is here — design, scan, and share QR codes, all in one app.**

**Design**
- Fully customizable QR codes: body shapes, eye shapes, colors, gradients
- Add your own logo or pick from a built-in library
- Add captions and center text
- Live preview while you edit

**Scan**
- Fast, accurate QR scanner with haptic feedback
- Open links, copy text, or save scanned data

**Export & Share**
- Save as high-quality PNG
- Share directly to any app (WhatsApp, Gmail, Drive, etc.)
- Your designs stay on your device — private by default

**Built for simplicity**
- Works completely offline
- No account, no signup, no tracking
- Light & dark themes
- Available in multiple languages

Free to download. No ads.
```
